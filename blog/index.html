Author: Lisa McMann

Series: The Unwanteds

Published: August 30, 2011

The Unwanteds

In the land of Quill, creativity is a crime, and those labeled Unwanteds are sent to their deaths. But when twelve-year-old Alex Stowe is sent away, he discovers a hidden world where imagination thrives under the guidance of the mysterious Mr. Today. This review explores Lisa McMann’s magical storytelling, themes of individuality, and the balance between logic and creativity.
