Sweet Potato Waffles

Prep: 15 min • Cook: 15 min • Serves: 4

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Crispy‑outside, tender‑inside waffles made with mashed sweet potato, warm spices, and just enough sweetness. Great with butter, syrup, or whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all‑purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup mashed cooked sweet potato
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tbsp melted butter or oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat waffle iron according to manufacturer instructions.
  2. Whisk flour, baking powder, salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, and brown sugar in a bowl.
  3. In another bowl, whisk milk, mashed sweet potato, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla until smooth.
  4. Pour wet ingredients into dry and stir just until combined.
  5. Lightly grease the waffle iron and cook batter in batches until waffles are golden and crisp.

Chicken Escalopes with Cream Sauce

Prep: 15 min • Cook: 20 min • Serves: 4

★ ★ ★ ★ ☆

Thin, pan‑seared chicken escalopes served in a silky cream sauce with garlic and herbs. Delicious over potatoes, rice, or pasta.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ lb chicken breast, sliced into thin escalopes
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • ¼ cup flour, for dredging
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 small shallot or onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • ¾ cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard (optional)
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley

Directions

  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper, then lightly coat in flour, shaking off excess.
  2. Heat olive oil and 1 tbsp butter in a skillet over medium‑high heat. Brown chicken 2–3 minutes per side; remove and keep warm.
  3. Reduce heat to medium. Add remaining butter and sauté shallot until soft, then add garlic and cook 30 seconds.
  4. Pour in chicken broth and simmer, scraping up browned bits. Stir in cream and Dijon; simmer until slightly thickened.
  5. Return chicken to the pan and simmer a few minutes more until cooked through. Sprinkle with parsley and serve.

Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

Prep: 15 min • Bake: 10–12 min • Makes: ~24 cookies

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Soft‑centered cookies with crisp edges and plenty of melty chocolate chips.

Ingredients

  • 2 ¼ cups all‑purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line baking sheets with parchment.
  2. Cream butter and sugars until light; beat in eggs and vanilla.
  3. Whisk flour, baking soda, and salt; mix into wet ingredients just until combined.
  4. Stir in chocolate chips. Scoop dough onto baking sheets.
  5. Bake 10–12 minutes, until edges are lightly golden. Cool on a rack.
