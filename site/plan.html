Sales Objection Trainer

WDD 131 – Final Project Site Plan

Author: Jaxon Becker

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to help sales professionals practice and improve their objection-handling skills through an interactive flashcard-style training experience. It provides real-world objections (“It’s too expensive”, “I need to talk to my wife”) and gives conversational, natural responses that build confidence and close more sales.

Intended Audience

This site is designed for retail sales professionals, especially new or developing salespeople who want to improve communication and sales techniques in a practical, interactive way.

Dynamic Elements (JavaScript Usage)

JavaScript will be used to create an interactive flashcard trainer where users can:

  • Flip objection cards to reveal responses (DOM manipulation)
  • Navigate through card sets using “Next” and “Previous” buttons
  • Mark cards as “Got it” or “Needs Work” using conditional logic
  • Track progress using arrays, objects, and ES modules
  • Dynamically display stats (how many cards mastered vs. need review)

Logo

Logo concept: A handshake icon or speech bubble to represent conversations and sales confidence.

Color Palette

Professional and confidence-building colors

  • #1E1E1E – Rich Black (header/nav)
  • #D4AF37 – Gold Accent (buttons, highlights)
  • #FFFFFF – White (background)
  • #F4F4F4 – Light Gray (content sections)

Fonts

Headings: Poppins or Montserrat (bold, modern)
Body Text: Open Sans or Roboto (clean and readable)

Content Plan

Home Page (index.html)

  • What the site does and why it matters
  • Hero image showing sales interaction
  • Flashcard trainer section with interactive JavaScript

Child Page (playbook.html)

  • Written objection scripts & talk-tracks
  • Sample responses with bullet-point follow-up questions
  • Conversation-flow examples
