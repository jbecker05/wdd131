How this site helps you sell more

Sales conversations can feel awkward when a customer throws out an objection you’re not ready for. This site gives you a low-pressure way to practice so you’re not guessing in front of a real customer.

You’ll work through common objections, see proven responses, and customize them to match your style. Use the cards during your downtime at work or at home to keep your skills sharp.

  • Practice common objections in a flashcard format
  • Read longer scripts and talk tracks in the playbook
  • Track which objections you’ve mastered and which need work
